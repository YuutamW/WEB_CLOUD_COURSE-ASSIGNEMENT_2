About

We've spent years obsessing over switch types, acoustic signatures, and uncompromising build quality. The QWERTY Shop was founded on a simple principle: your primary tool for interacting with the digital world shouldn't feel like an afterthought. It should be a precision instrument tailored exactly to your preferences.

Whether you're compiling heavy C++ projects, navigating WSL terminals, or building custom rendering engines from scratch, every keystroke matters. Our custom-built mechanical boards feature hot-swappable switches and ergonomic layouts designed to provide the tactile feedback needed to keep your hands comfortable through thousands of lines of code.

And when it's time to log off and unwind, these boards transition seamlessly to high-stakes tactical gaming. From quick peeks in Battlefield to perfectly timed extractions after securing rare loot like a pristine cassette tape, you'll have the lightning-fast responsiveness required to perform at your absolute best.
